Huyen Tran's Education Journey

Early Education

Huyen Tran began her educational journey with a strong focus on science and mathematics, excelling in her studies and showing a keen interest in technology.

Undergraduate Studies

Huyen pursued a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, where she developed a deep understanding of programming, data structures, and algorithms.

Specialized Training

After completing her undergraduate degree, Huyen enrolled in specialized courses in cybersecurity and network security, gaining practical skills that would later define her career in InfoSec.

Professional Certifications

To further establish her expertise, Huyen attained several key certifications in the field, including the Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP) and the Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH).

Ongoing Learning

Committed to continuous improvement, Huyen regularly participates in workshops and seminars to stay updated with the latest trends and technologies in information security.
